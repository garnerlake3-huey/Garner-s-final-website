Why I Play

I am very passionate about the sport of basketball, which I have been playing since the age of 5. It has helped me meet my closest friends, develop a competitive mindset, and look to excel in something I truly love!

Beyond just a game, it's a discipline. The hours spent in the gym, the early mornings, and the teamwork required to win have shaped my work ethic in every other area of life, from school to business.

My Training Routine

To get better, I follow a specific plan:

  1. Warm-up & Stretching
  2. Skill Development
    • Ball Handling (15 mins)
    • Shooting Form (Free throws & Mid-range)
    • 3-Point Repetitions (100 makes)
  3. Strength & Conditioning
  4. Scrimmage / Live Play
